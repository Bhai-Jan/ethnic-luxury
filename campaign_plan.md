# Digital Campaign Plan: Ethnic Luxury Launch (Updated)

## 1. Overview
**Campaign Goal**: Establish Ethnic Luxury as the premier destination for authentic, cultural fashion in Sweden, emphasizing **variety, ease of discovery, and cultural pride**.
**Primary Metric**: Customer Club Sign-ups and First Purchases.

## 2. Updated Platform Strategy
### Instagram & TikTok (Visual Discovery)
-   **Content**: "How to Style" reels featuring the new product categories (e.g., "Styling a Pakistani Kurta for a Swedish Wedding").
-   **Interactive Stories**: Polls asking "Which region's style do you want to see next?" to drive traffic to the new "Featured Collections" section.
-   **Influencer Unboxing**: Collaborators showing the quality of the fabrics up close, leveraging the new "zoom" feature on the site as a talking point.

### Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
-   **Keywords**: Targeted campaigns for specific items now available in the expanded catalog: "Köp Sari Sverige," "Dashiki Online," "Afghan Dress Sweden."
-   **Landing Pages**: Direct traffic to the filtered views (e.g., ads for saris link directly to `?filter=sari`—simulated).

## 3. Key Messaging & Creative
**Headline**: "Your Heritage, Delivered." / "Ditt Arv, Levererat."
**Sub-headline**: "Explore 100+ authentic styles from Pakistan, Africa, and the Middle East."
**Call to Action (CTA)**:
-   "Search Your Style & Save 15%."
-   "Join the Club for Exclusive Drops."

## 4. Paid Advertising (PPC) Strategy
-   **Retargeting**: Users who viewed specific product categories (e.g., Kaftans) but didn't buy will see carousel ads featuring those specific items.
-   **Lookalike Audiences**: Target users similar to our current "Customer Club" members.

## 5. Metrics for Success (KPIs)
-   **Search Usage**: >20% of visitors using the search bar or filters (indicates high intent).
-   **Time on Site**: Increase by 30% due to expanded catalog and interactive features.
-   **Club Conversion**: 5% of unique visitors joining the club.
-   **Bilingual Engagement**: Monitor usage of the EN/SV toggle to better tailor future ad copy language.

## 6. Budget Allocation
-   **Social Ads (Visuals)**: 50%
-   **Google Shopping/Search**: 30% (Capturing high-intent searches for specific items like "Sari")
-   **Influencer Partnerships**: 20%
