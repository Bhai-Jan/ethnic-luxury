Our Community

Ethnic Luxury is a home for women in Sweden who carry the vibrant heritage of Asia and Africa in their hearts. Whether you are looking for a saree for a wedding, a comfortable kurta for Eid, or simply want to express your identity through fashion, we are here for you.

Founded by a diverse team with roots in Pakistan, Afghanistan, the Middle East, and Africa, we understand the challenge of finding authentic cultural wear in Scandinavia. We exist to make that search effortless.

Celebrating Diversity

Conscious Fashion, Minimal Waste

We are committed to a smarter way of shopping. By utilizing a **direct-to-consumer dropshipping model**, we eliminate the need for massive warehouses and unsold inventory that plagues the fast fashion industry.


Every item is shipped only when you order it, significantly reducing textile waste and carbon footprint associated with overproduction. We curate small, meaningful collections rather than chasing fleeting trends, ensuring that our partners—local artisans and suppliers—are respected and your clothes are made with care.
